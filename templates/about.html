💡 Our Story

Why we built MindEase

During our mid-term examinations, we were juggling hackathons, academics, and internship responsibilities all at once. The pressure felt overwhelming — and there was no one readily available to talk to.

We talked to friends in our batch and they said the same thing — mentally tired, but nowhere to go. Most students don't want to discuss mental health openly because of the stigma around it.

That's why we built MindEase — a private, anonymous space where students can check in with their mental health, get AI-powered stress predictions, and access curated resources. No judgment. No login. Just support.
